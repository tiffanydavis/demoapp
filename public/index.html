Hey girls! Throw a spin on newspaper nails and pair it with this summers hot new trend - NEON NAILS! Personally, I LOVE neon and am super happy its back, in the words of Heidi Klume, IN again. So I already had the Essie Funky Limelight polish circa 2009 in my collection. If you can't find it out there, any neon will do, but i recommend staying with lighter colors. I tried it with green and the newspaper print didn't show well.
